THE BANE OF
BOBBY BOY





























INSTRUCTIONS

Bobby Boy, a once-cheerful soul with dreams as big as the open sky, has fallen into a shadowy realm of despair. Life's relentless hardships have chipped away at his spirit, leaving him isolated and lost in a maze of self-doubt. With every passing day, his world grows darker, and he struggles to find meaning in a life that feels like a constant uphill battle. In this gripping journey, it’s up to you, the player, to step into Bobby Boy’s shoes and guide him through the storm. Unravel the pieces of his past, face his inner demons, and help him rediscover the light within. Will you be the one to lift Bobby Boy out of his abyss and help him rewrite his story? The fate of his happiness lies in your hands.




MEET THE OBSTACLES!

Players must navigate five obstacles before achieving their goal:

1. Procrastination Pit: Jump over or fall in, slowing progress.
2. Doubt Mirrors: Distorted reflections make it hard to see the right path.
3. Excuse Chains: Players must break chains symbolizing excuses.
4. Overthinking Maze: A maze that punishes players for indecision.
5. Fear Cliff: Requires precise timing to jump across.
